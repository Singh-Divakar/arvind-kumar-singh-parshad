From the Parshad

Message to Citizens

Dear Residents of Ward No. 1,

You gave me your trust — and that trust is the greatest honour of my life.

Every road I helped pave, every drain I helped clear, every family I helped connect to a government scheme — none of it would have been possible without your support, your patience, and your faith in me.

Ward No. 1 is not just an administrative unit on a map. It is our home. It is where our children play, our elders rest, and our families grow. I promise you — I will never let politics, personal gain, or bureaucratic delays come in the way of what this home deserves.

I am your neighbour before I am your Parshad. My door is always open. My phone is always on. And my commitment to this ward will never waver.

Together, we will build a ward that becomes an example for all of Gorakhpur.

Jai Hind. Jai Gorakhpur.

— Arvind Singh
Parshad, Ward No. 1 (Mahadev Jharkhandi Tukda No-1), Gorakhpur Nagar Nigam
