About the Leader

Who is Arvind Kumar Singh?

Arvind Kumar Singh is a dedicated, people-first public representative serving as the elected Parshad of Ward No. 01 under Gorakhpur Nagar Nigam. With deep roots in the community and an unwavering commitment to civic development, he has emerged as a trusted voice for the residents of his ward.

His approach to governance is simple yet powerful — listen first, act fast, and deliver with accountability. From unpaved lanes to blocked drains, from unlit streets to unresolved civic complaints, he has positioned himself as an accessible, responsive, and hardworking representative who believes that real change begins at the grassroots level.

"Ward No. 01 should be a model ward — clean, well-connected, well-lit, and safe for every resident, especially women, elderly citizens, and children."
